Kinn Kinn Menu Board

Client Project / Menu Design, Branding
Overview

Kinn Kinn (กินน์) is a restaurant in Cebu City, Philippines. They focus on providing affordable, authentic, and tasty international foods with a spotlight on Thai cuisine. For their launch, there was an important asset they needed—their menu, of course!

Duration

October 2024 to October 2024

Roles

Menu Design, Branding

Tools

Figma, Canva

Objective

A menu board for physical and digital use

After working on the logo and visual identity for Kinn Kinn, I was contacted soon after for another task. This next project was to create a vertical orientation menu board of Kinn Kinn’s food and beverage offerings.

Menu Design Process

Larger than life

The dimensions for Kinn Kinn’s menu design measured to be 4’ x 3’. Up until this point, I had never worked on something of this scale so I was really excited to see how it would turn out at the end.

I started with layout sketches on paper before trying out some iterations digitally. Once a layout was decided upon, I worked to finalize two versions of the menu, one with a placeholder for a QR code and one without.

Final Thoughts

A tasteful design for delicious eats

Seeing the final photos of the printed menu board put up on the exterior of Kinn Kinn’s shipping container restaurant was surreal. One day, I’d like to visit Kinn Kinn’s location in the Philippines to see the menu board in real life and to order a dish from it!

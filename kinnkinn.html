Kinn Kinn Visual Identity

Client Project / Logo Design, Branding
Overview

Kinn Kinn (กินน์) is a restaurant in Cebu City, Philippines that needed a logo and other assets for their launch. They focus on providing affordable, authentic, and tasty international foods with a spotlight on Thai cuisine.

Duration

August 2024 to September 2024

Roles

Logo Design, Branding

Tools

Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop, Figma, Canva

Objective

Making a great first impression

The main goal of this project was to create a modern, trendy, and authentic brand logo, style guide, and social media assets to aid Kinn Kinn’s launch.

Logo Design WIP

The face of Kinn Kinn

One of the mandatories for the logo design was to have a mascot. Since Thai cuisine is Kinn Kinn’s focus, I looked into different animals and mythological creatures that related to Thai culture.

In the end, once the top sketches were brought for review and the rationale behind each option was explained, it was the strong and reliable buffalo that became Kinn Kinn’s mascot.

Social Media WIP

Putting things together

Kinn Kinn’s social media assets included some brand elements and templates for the initial launch. The templates, when put together in a layout that resembled Instagram’s feed, turned out cohesive and quite poppy.

Aside from the usual inclusions in a style guide—colours, typefaces, logo variations, etc.—photography art direction was also added. This way, the photographer would have a clearer vision for shooting the food photography later on.

Reflection

Bringing a brand to life

This project taught me a lot about Thai culture during the initial research and ideation phase. It was also such a great experience working with a client who was on the other side of the world from me (there was a 16-hour time difference).

Breathing life into my Kinn Kinn’s brand was an amazing opportunity. As I was compiling the style guide and bringing everything together at the end of this project, I remember the immense accomplishment and excitement before I sent it off for final approval. This was such a fun project to work on!
